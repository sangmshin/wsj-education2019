FAQ

How do I find out if my school offers complimentary membership?

Use the search school feature at education.wsj.com/search.

How can professors from non-partner universities join WSJ?

Professors may qualify for a 6-month complimentary membership by integrating WSJ in their courses. More information about the WSJ Referral program can be found at wsj.com/refer. Alternatively, professors may purchase their own individual membership by visiting subscribe.wsj.com/professor.

What is the WSJ Referral Program?

The WSJ Referral Program allows professors to easily refer students to the Journal. Once your students join, you can earn a 6-month complimentary membership.

How do I sign up for the WSJ Referral Program?

Visit wsj.com/refer and create an account. Please note that you will need to use your .edu email address when signing up.

Is there a place where I can quickly find useful articles for case studies?

You can find case studies with premade questions for the classroom in the “Case Studies” section.

How can I integrate WSJ into my course?

There are how-to guides on WSJ Education dedicated to helping professors incorporate the Journal into their curriculum, including advice from other professors.

What is Context?

Context is the Journal’s first higher education-focused product, allowing instructors to seamlessly integrate WSJ content into their courses through shared reading lists.

How do I access Context?

Context is included with all WSJ Education memberships and you can create your account here.

What is the Weekly Review Archive?

The Weekly Review Archive is a searchable database of past Journal articles that helps you identify content ideally suited to each week's lesson. It includes more than a decade’s worth of articles searchable by discipline, topic and keyword. Each article includes a summary and discussion questions for easy course integration. You can access the archive here.

Are WSJ-School Sponsored Memberships available outside of the US?

Yes, they are available internationally. Please contact partnersubs@dowjones.com for more details.

How do I contact Customer Service regarding issues with my WSJ account?

You can contact our Customer Service team by calling 1-800-JOURNAL.
